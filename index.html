Oh, Hey There!

My name is Shelagh (like Sheila!), and I am a Web Developer based in Calgary, Alberta.

I started playing with web design and development when I was 13, thinking I was so cool with my simple websites hosted on the Angelfire or GeoCities platforms. I learned HTML and CSS and built layouts in Photoshop for those beautiful frame-based websites of the late 90s, early 2000s!

After persuing a different career in the travel industry for the better part of a decade, I decided to return to my original passion. I dusted off my coding skills and began learning the new tricks of the trade through online bootcamps and tutorials.

Currently, I am expanding these skills in SAIT's Web Developer program.
